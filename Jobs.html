About us

We are a highly experienced team with a unique approach to making games. Our offices are located in Tampere, Finland. Currently we create new and unique games for console and PC platforms.

Our working environment is very relaxed and the team works in tight cooperation with each other. Everyone takes their responsibilities seriously and works hard to achieve our common goals. As gamers we hope you have the passion for gaming as well.

We regularly have after work board game events, but that is of course voluntary. If you think we are someone you would like to work with, you've come to the right page.
