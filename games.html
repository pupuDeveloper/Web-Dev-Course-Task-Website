Rat Astronauts: the Cheese quest

Rat Astronauts is our work in progress project that stars a crew of rat astronauts on their quest to the moon. Rat astronauts is our first shot at mobile gaming. We wish that our fans are able to enjoy our games wherever they are. This doesn't mean we are forgetting our PC games. PC still remains our main domain. Thanks to your support we have been able to branch out to new fields, like mobile gaming. Be sure to follow our twitter and join our discord server to stay updated on Rat Astronauts!
